Frequently Asked Questions

Expert guidance for luxury collectors

What makes MASUKAME hinoki wooden sculptures special?

MASUKAME sculptures are handcrafted from premium Japanese hinoki cypress by master artisans. Each piece combines the prosperity symbol (Masu) with longevity (Kame), creating a unique luxury art piece that embodies traditional Japanese craftsmanship and cultural significance. The hinoki wood is carefully selected for its fine grain, natural fragrance, and spiritual importance in Japanese culture.

How should I care for my hinoki wooden sculpture?

Keep your MASUKAME sculpture away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Avoid exposure to water or high humidity. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. The natural hinoki fragrance will gradually soften over time, which is normal and preserves the wood's integrity. For long-term preservation, maintain stable room temperature and humidity levels.

What is the investment value of MASUKAME sculptures?

MASUKAME sculptures are limited edition, made-to-order pieces with unique serial numbers and certificates of authenticity. The combination of premium hinoki wood, master craftsmanship, and cultural significance makes them valuable collectibles that appreciate over time. Each piece represents both artistic heritage and tangible investment for discerning collectors.

How long does it take to create a MASUKAME sculpture?

Each MASUKAME sculpture requires 6-8 weeks of handcrafting by skilled artisans. This includes wood selection, carving, finishing, and quality inspection. The extended timeframe ensures exceptional quality and attention to detail worthy of luxury collectors. Rush orders cannot be accommodated due to the meticulous nature of the craftsmanship.

Do you ship MASUKAME sculptures internationally?

Yes, we offer secure worldwide shipping for MASUKAME sculptures. All shipments include full insurance and tracking. Shipping is handled by trusted partners experienced with luxury art pieces to ensure safe delivery to collectors globally. Delivery times vary by destination, typically 5-10 business days after completion.

What comes with each MASUKAME sculpture?

Each MASUKAME sculpture includes a hand-signed certificate of authenticity with unique serial number, detailed care instructions, and elegant packaging suitable for gifting. The certificate provides provenance documentation important for collectors and insurance purposes. We also include information about the specific hinoki wood source and artisan details.

Can I customize my MASUKAME sculpture?

While MASUKAME maintains its core design integrity, minor customizations such as finish preference (oil or urethane) can be specified during ordering. Each piece is individually crafted, resulting in natural variations that make every sculpture unique. Major design modifications are not available to preserve the artistic vision and cultural authenticity.

What is the significance of hinoki wood in Japanese culture?

Hinoki cypress is considered sacred in Japanese culture and has been used for centuries in temple construction and ceremonial objects. Its natural antibacterial properties, fine grain, and distinctive fragrance make it highly prized. MASUKAME sculptures honor this tradition while creating contemporary art pieces that bridge cultural heritage with modern luxury collecting.

Are there size variations available?

MASUKAME sculptures are crafted at approximately 10cm in height and width, representing the optimal proportions for the masu and kame design elements. This size was specifically chosen to showcase the intricate details while remaining suitable for display in luxury home environments. Custom sizing is not currently available to maintain design integrity.

How do I verify the authenticity of my MASUKAME sculpture?

Each authentic MASUKAME sculpture includes a hand-signed certificate with unique serial number that can be verified through our records. The sculpture itself bears subtle maker marks known only to our artisans. For verification inquiries, contact us with your certificate number and purchase details through our secure channels.
